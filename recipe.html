Our Classic Artisan French Fries



serves 4-6 people
prep time: 20 minutes, 1 hour 20 minutes total
ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 4 to 5 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 teaspoons paprika


directions:
  1. peel potatoes
  2. cut the potatoes lengthwise into thin strips,
    about 1/3-inch wide.
  3. soak the potatoes in ice-cold water for 1 hour.
  4. drain and pat completely dry.
  5. heat the oil in a deep-fryer or deep skillet to
    325 F and blanchthe potatoes by frying them in
    batches for 2 minutes. Drain the fries on fresh
    kitchen towels or paper towels.
  6. increase the heat of the oil to 375 F then cook
    the potatoes for the second time in batches for
    1 1/2 minutes.
  7. drain the fries on baking sheets lined with paper
    towels and portion them on servings plates.
  8. sprinkle them with salt and paprika, serve
    immediately.
  9. enjoy!!

Worchestershire French Fries



serves 4-6 people
prep time: 30 minutes, 1 hour 30 minutes total
ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 4 to 5 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ground black pepper to taste


directions:
  1. peel potatoes
  2. cut the potatoes lengthwise into thin strips,
    about 1/3-inch wide.
  3. soak the potatoes in ice-cold water for 1 hour.
  4. drain and pat completely dry.
  5. heat the oil in a deep-fryer or deep skillet to
    325 F and blanchthe potatoes by frying them in
    batches for 2 minutes. Drain the fries on fresh
    kitchen towels or paper towels.
  6. increase the heat of the oil to 375 F then cook
    the potatoes for the second time in batches for
    1 1/2 minutes.
  7. drain the fries on baking sheets lined with paper
    towels and portion them on servings plates.
  8. combine ingredients 4 - 12 in a sauce pan. bring
    to boil. Let chill for 5 minutes.
  9. toss the fries in a bowl with the worchestershire
    sauce, serve immediately.
  10. enjoy!!
