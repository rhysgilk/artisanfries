Artisan Fries has been a Boston area favorite since 1986 when mentors offered sage advice to the five young Tufts students: "Start a business or go to college." They selected the business route, opening an artisinal french fry store in Sudbury. Under the guidance of world renowned French Fry Artists, they cooked and prepared fresh-cut fries to perfection.

Now, 30 years after Artisan Fries first opened, there are multiple locations in the Boston area and another two new store fronts in development. Artisan Fries continues to receive generous media attention and has grown a cult-like following around Boston, maybe even nationally.
